What are Wildfires?

Wildfires are naturally occuring phenomenon which can be devastate large areas of land causing sever damage to community infrastucture resources and livestock.

Why do they occur?

Wildfires are often caused directly by the global warming effect. They occur more commonly in climates which are arid and dry. Wildifres can also be caused by human activity, intentional or otherwise.
